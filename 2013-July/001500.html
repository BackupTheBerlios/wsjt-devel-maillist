On another matter, the question of startup default frequencies:

After receiving a bunch of input from the wsjtgroup reflector, as well 
as off-list, I've concluded that the best frequencies are probably these:

Band   JT65
--------------
  160   1.838
   80   3.576
   60   5.357
   40   7.076
   30  10.138
   20  14.076
   17  18.102
   15  21.076
   12  24.917
   10  28.076
    6  50.276

For narrow-band, JT9-only operation, add 2 kHz.  (That's what the new 
checbox is for, of course.)

Regarding 30m: I've inserted what might be called a "software notch 
filter".  If you try to transmit in the WSPR band, the program will 
refuse and pop up this message:

"Please choose another Tx frequency.
  WSJT-X will not knowingly transmit
  between 10.139 900 and 10.140 320 MHz,
  the 30 m sub-band for WSPR transmissions."

This enforces a small "hole" in the WSJT-X displayed portion of the 30m 
band, set aside for use by WSPR.  Rather conveniently, it's located 
between the JT65 and JT9 segments: from 1900 to 2320 Hz on your WSJT-X 
waterfall.  (The blanked-out width is 320 Hz -- the 200 Hz width of the 
WSPR band, plus the 177 Hz width of a JT65 signal, plus a 20 Hz guard 
band on each end.)
