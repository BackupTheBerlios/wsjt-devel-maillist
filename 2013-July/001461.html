Hi Joe and others
 
I think there should be some indicator on the main window that WSJTXv1.1 is trying to use "SPLIT"
frequencies or some way to stop the use even when using CAT/CIV control.
I have sometimes been unable to make any QSOs at times only to find that I was sending at
a frequency 1kHz higher than the receive frequency.
 
It is not  obvious on my radio that the VFO has been changed while transmitting.
This may well be a design flaw with the Icom radios I use but it would be less confusing if there was some
indicator on the WSJTX window.
