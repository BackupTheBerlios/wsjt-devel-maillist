Hi Edson,

I was about to ask whether you had set the "JT65 nnnn JT9" spinner 
correctly.  Its purpose is described in the "Quick-Start Guide to WSJT-X 
Version 1.1"
http://www.physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/WSJTX_1.1_Quick_Start.pdf
  which is also accessible from the Help menu.

Simply put: the number specifies the audio frequency dividing the JT65 
decoding region from the JT9 region.

     -- Joe, K1JT
