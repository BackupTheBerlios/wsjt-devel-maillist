Hi Edson,

What is the dial frequency of your receiver?   Although the band menu specifies 14.078 for 20m, the scheme with the new wsjtx with combined operations for jt65 and jt9 requires that RX frequency should be 14.076 (VFO A) and split TX frequency (VFO B) set to 14.078.   Then the JT65-JT9 split at 3000 will work.
