Josh --

I've never spent significant time checking out "Multiple Instances", and 
in fact it was turned off (the menu item can be checked, but it does 
nothing) in r3482.

If you want to try again, I've re-enabled it for you.  Download the main 
executable
http://www.physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/wsjtx.exe
and put copies in each of your four directories.

You can think of this one as revision 3482a.  It's exactly the same as 
r3482, except that the "Multiple Instances" option was reconnected.

I just checked it here with two instances, and they both seem to work OK.

Let me know what results you get... and maybe also, explain why anyone 
might want to do this?

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
