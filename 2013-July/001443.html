You'll find the explanation in item 1, page 4 of
http://www.physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/WSJT-X_1.1_Quick_Start.pdf

If you're using the wideband capability of WSJT-X v1.1 -- more bandwidth 
than a standard SSB filter -- your transceiver should be in Split mode. 
  Then, suppose the Rx dial frequency (VFO_A) is 14.076.

To transmit at a frequency below 1000 Hz on the waterfall, VFO_B will be
set to 14.075.

To transmit between 1000 and 2000 Hz on the waterfall, VFO_B = 14.076.
To transmit between 2000 and 3000 Hz on the waterfall, VFO_B = 14.077.
To transmit between 3000 and 4000 Hz on the waterfall, VFO_B = 14.078.

	-- Joe, K1JT
