Hi John and all,

G4KLA wrote:
> Another point concerning rigs with no CAT control, as well as those that do, what frequencies should be listed under the Band menu?  At present, selecting 20m with CAT control will change RX to 14.078 when 14.076 is needed.

At present, the default dial frequencies are those we suggested for JT9, 
back when the assumed JT9 sub-band was 1 kHz wide.  You can edit these 
frequencies (Setup | Configuration | Default Frequencies), and the 
program will remember your edits on restart.  In some ways, this seems 
best to me -- since different stations may want different default 
frequencies (depending on available bandwidth, for example).

Yes, it might be better to have the original startup defaults set to the 
JT65 frequencies.  On the other hand, JT65 is already well entrenched... 
and one of the goals is to let the advantages of JT9 over JT65 be made 
evident to all.

In general, I think such matters are best decided by "the community".

I would appreciate hearing any advice and opinions on this matter from 
others.

	-- Joe, K1JT
