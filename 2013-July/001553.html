Thanks for trying that out. That makes sense now, there will be no comms 
possible in hardware handshake mode unless the radio cooperates so that 
is good evidence that your radio requires hardware handshaking.

With RTS forced on and no handshaking there would be a possibility of 
data overruns which would be bad.

I think you are right to use the hardware handshaking option with your 
radio configuration.
