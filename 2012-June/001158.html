Hi Guy,

MAP65 probably thinks you are operating on 28 MHz, or whatever your IF 
is, because Linrad sends frequency information to MAP65 along with the 
baseband data.

Try putting "144.125" (your center frequency) in the box at lower right 
of the Wide Graph, and check the box "Force Center Freq (MHz)".

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
