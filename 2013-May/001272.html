Hi Joe,

I did some checks and found out that the read() timeout is working under
linux. The GUI does not freeze when I turn off the radio and everything is
restored when I turn the radio back on. One thing I've learned by looking
at the hamlib sources is that the serial communication routines could be
different depending on the radio model and maker. Implementation is also
different for Windows and POSIX systems This could present some
difficulties in troubleshooting problems as things could work well for some
and not for others (as we are seeing on the wsjt discussion list).

73,

-- Edson PY2SDR
