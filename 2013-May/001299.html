Hi Edson and all,

I have been building WSJT-X with Qt 4.7 or 4.8.  Today I installed Qt 
5.0.2 and played with it long enough to hit a dead end (for now). 
Here's what I did, and what happened:

1. Added "QT += widgets" in wsjtx.pro

2. Replaced "#include <QtGui>" with "#include <QtWidgets>" in logqso.h, 
main.cpp, mainwindow.h, and plotter.h

When I try to compile, the automatically generated files ui_*.h contain 
many statements of the form

#include <QtGui/Qsomething...>

These should probably be "<QtWidgets/Qsomething...>" , but why doesn't 
QtCreator (or qmake, or ... ) know this?

So, for now I am back to Qt 4.x.

	-- Joe
