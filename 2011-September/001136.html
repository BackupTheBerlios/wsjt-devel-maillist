The Berlios repository has served the needs of the WSJT/MAP65/WSPR 
project very well since WSJT first became an open source project. 
However, you have probably heard that the Berlios hosting services will 
end on December 31, 2011.

I have started the process of moving our source code from Berlios to 
SourceForge.  I hope this change will be relatively painless for most of 
us.

For now, and until further notice, please do not commit any cource code 
changes without asking me first.

Assuming that you would like to continue your present association with 
the WSJT project(s). I would ask that you register for a SourceForge 
account at https://sourceforge.net/user/registration .  Then, please 
send me your SourceForge username.

I will hope to have further information on the transition by some time 
next week.

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
