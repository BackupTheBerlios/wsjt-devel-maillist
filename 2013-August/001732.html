Hi David, Edson, and all,

Happy to hear that you like the new waterfall palettes!

> A suggestion: What do you think of moving the waterfall configuration
> controls to a dedicated tab in setup/configuration? I believe once the
> waterfall parameters are set, they are rarely touched.

Maybe that would be good.  It would be nice to go back to just one row 
of controls on the waterfall.

Two counter-arguments:

1.  Other things being equal, controls should be located close to where 
their effect takes place.

2. Program code would be slightly more messy (i.e., spread over several 
files) if it involves devsetup, mainwindow, and widegraph instead of 
widegraph alone.

I don't have strong feelings about this, either way.  If others think 
the selection would be better on the configuration screen, that's OK 
with me.
