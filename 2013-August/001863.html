Hi John, Bill, and all,

Thanks for your comments and ideas about audio input matters.

I have thought for quite some time that it would probably make life 
simpler if our audio input, like the audio output, were always done at 
48000 samples per second.  We would then do the relatively simple 
resample task (from 48000 to 12000 Hz) in our own code, rather than 
relying on the system to do it.  As far as I know all recent sound cards 
are capably of 48000 Hz sampling, and this way we would have control 
over the quality of the resampling.  WSPR has done it this way, since 
several years ago.  It works well.
