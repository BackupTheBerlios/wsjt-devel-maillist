Hi Bill,

My Windows system also reports that same list of supported sample rates, 
but it doesn't seem to matter.  As you can see in my sample QAudio code, 
I just go ahead and request sample rate 12000:

   audioFormat.setSampleRate(12000);

and then find that

   audioFormat.isValid()

returns "true".

The resulting stream starts without error and runs properly.
