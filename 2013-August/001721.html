Hi all,

Here's another idea related to Tx Audio.

When operating in "Split Tx" more we now adjust the nominal frequency of 
the generated audio signal (actually the frequency of the sync tone) so 
that it falls in the range 1000 - 2000 Hz.  The transceiver Tx dial 
frequency is offset in 1000 Hz steps to accommodate the adjustment.

I think it would be better to limit the generated audio  to an even 
smaller range, say 1500 - 2000 Hz.  Harmonics of the generated audio 
would then fall outside the transceiver's Tx filter.  Signals would be 
much cleaner in cases where the user has set the Tx audio level too high.

Comments?
