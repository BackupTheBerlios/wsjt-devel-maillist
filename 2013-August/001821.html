without that additional package no audio devices are listed. The small
"test" program does demonstrate the same behavior.

installed on my 13.04 system.

activity with "top", and saw that Xorg was hogging the CPU. It was
because I had left pavucontrol running and its realtime updating of
its VU meters is extremely X-intensive!
