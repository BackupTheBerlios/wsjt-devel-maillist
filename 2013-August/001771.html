I've just made another small update.  The width of the text browser and 
the font metrics are used to determine how many characters are available 
for the decoded text line.  When DXCC entities are added they are 
truncated to prevent the text from being moved to the left mucking up 
the columns.

The text browser accepts a subset of HTML but would appear to ignore 
align=left which would have been a much simpler fix.  That maybe due to 
using preformatted text or maybe just a limitation of the implementation.

Either way, the font size and/or the window size can now be changed 
without destroying the formatting.
