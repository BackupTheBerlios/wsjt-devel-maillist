Probably you're way ahead of me on this, but just in case:

On the system I'm using now for tests, using code revision 3528:

1. Start program
2. Click "Tune", listen to Tx audio
3. Hit "F2" to open the Configuration screen.

I hear glitches in the Tx audio every time at step 3.

By default, Qt has set the buffer size to 19200.  Now, insert

   m_stream->setBufferSize(bufSize);

at line 71 of soundout.cpp, just before

   m_stream->start (source);

With bufSize=24000 I still hear glitches at step 3.
With bufsize=32000 the glitches are gone.

On the basis of this simple test. I'd suggest setting bufSize=48000, say.
