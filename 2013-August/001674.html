Hi Bill and all,

I have WSJT-X running well on both Windows and Linux, using r3522 plus 
Bill's patch.  It's QAudio all the way, no PortAudio.  To make it work 
in Linux I had to remove the "#ifdef UNIX" blocks in both Modulator.cpp 
and soundout.cpp that dealt withe the PortAudio quirk of insisting on 
sending audio output to both Left and Right channels.

I did have some hassles getting it to run in Linux.  With the exception 
noted above, they all had to do with (a) having several versions of Qt 
(4.7, 5.0.2, 5.1.0) installed and not having set some environment 
variables properly.  I'm learning Qt's behavior and requirements, albeit 
rather slowly.  I will gradually move everything in my development 
machines (two for Windows, two more for Linux) to Qt 5.1.

Bill, your code appears to be solid, and I'm starting to learn my way 
around it.

Many of your changes make me feel like a student who just had his paper 
returned with lots of red ink provided by his conscientious teacher.  I 
will study, and learn.

I'm looking forward to reports from others!

	-- Joe, K1JT
