Hi Bill,

Revision 3533 compiled and running nicely here.  (It calls itself r3532 
-- the displayed revision number, line 40 in mainwindow.cpp, gets 
updated only when mainwindow.cpp is committed.  Therefore we always try 
to remember to make at least a trivial change in mainwindow before doing 
a commit.  By convention, this is done by adding or removing a "-" in 
line #1.

One immediate question: why keep an unnecessary platform difference by 
having stereo sound in UNIX?  We did that before because PortAudio 
seemed to require it.  That's no longer an issue, so I suggest we always 
use single-channel output.

More comments to come, perhaps, after I make some QSOs.
