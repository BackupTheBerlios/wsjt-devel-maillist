Is anyone still here?

Last September I moved WSJT/MAP65/WSPR source code repositories from 
Berlios to SourceForge, because Berlios said they would end their 
open-source hosting of 12/31/2011.

Much to my dismay, SourceForge has corrupted our repositories several 
times.  I have never discovered exactly what went wrong.  Perhaps it's 
my fault; I really don't know.

Anyway, the folks at Berlios changed their minds and decided to remain 
open for business.  We found them very reliable, so I have now moved 
WSJT and WSPR back there.

Reminder: members of the development team can check out code with one of 
the following commands:

$ svn co https://loginname@svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/wsjt/trunk

$ svn co https://loginname@svn.berlios.de/svnroot/repos/wsjt/branches/wspr

Anyone can do an anonymous checkout with the commands

$ svn co svn://svn.berlios.de/wsjt/trunk

$ svn co svn://svn.berlios.de/wsjt/branches/wspr

If you're still subscribed to this list, and still interested in 
possible future contributions to development of WSJT and/or WSPR, could 
you please let me know by an off-list email?  Many thanks!

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
