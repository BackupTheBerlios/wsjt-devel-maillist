about a properly functioning Windows sound system, I know of no reason 
why it should not work.

> Another point I forgot to mention:
> The WSPR displayed DT, seems to have 1s offset:
> - using my 2 stations sync on the same clock through dimension4 I see a 
> constant DT+1 (or 0.9 to 1.1)
> - I see the same DT +1 on a large majority of stations
> I see the same, when time synchronized manually using a DCF77 clock or a 
> GPS (when I don't have Internet access)
> When I operate JT65, I see DT 0, most of the time...
> But I am sure you  noticed it...

The DT displayed by WSPR is biased by +1 s because the Tx Audio starts 
at t=1 s into an even minute.  Of course it would be trivial to remove 
this bias, and probably that should be done.  I guess I just never 
worried much about it.
