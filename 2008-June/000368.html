I just removed KVASD.EXE and then ran the standard (released) WSJT on 
the files in the Samples/JT65B directory.  The files named RU1AA* and 
RW1AY* decode perfectly with the BM algorithm, because those signals are 
relatively strong.  The other (non-shorthand) files decode only when 
Deep Search is enabled.  The KV algorithm has about a 2 dB advantage 
over the BM algorithm on the AWGN channel, and a much larger (up to 6 or 
7 dB) on a Rayleigh fading channel like EME.
