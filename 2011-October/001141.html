Your screen dump says that you tried to open device 5 for both input and 
output.

As stated in the first error message (and in the table of devices 
above), device 5 is not an input device.

As stated in the second error message, device 5 reports that it does not 
offer a sample rate of 11025 Hz.

Most likely, you can use the ".asoundrc" work-around described in the 
WSJT 9.0 Supplement to the User's Guide.  See page 8 of
http://www.physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/WSJT_9.0_Supplement.pdf .

For details, you're on your own.
