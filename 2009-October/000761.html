Hi Mike,

Thanks for your kind words about WSJT and WSPR.

Of course you are most welcome to obtain code from the SVN repositories 
and to share your experiences with it here.  Be forewarned, however, 
that WSPR is presently in a state of evolution leading to a version 2.0, 
probably not very far off.  It is NOT yet ready for routine use, 
however; and depending on just exactly which revision you check out, you 
may find some features broken or only partially implemented.

If I find time to work on it next week, the remaining necessities for 
proper serial-port use in WSPR will likely be completed.
