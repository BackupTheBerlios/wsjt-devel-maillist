Hi Jeff,

As I think you know, setting "Rx noise" to 0 dB nominally 
gives you the ideal level.

Under most conditions you have considerable leeway.  I think 
I set things so that 0 db corresponds to an rms level of 100 
in the 16-bit data coming into MAP65 from Linrad.  A level 
of +20 dB would mean rms=1000, which is still OK but reduces 
your "headroom" for handling very strong signals.

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
