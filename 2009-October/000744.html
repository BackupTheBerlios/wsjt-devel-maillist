Hi Zilvis,

I could not compile WSJT a long time under Debian. Now it compiles and 
works fine again. I use Debian Testing. It updates almost every  day so 
I did not mention after which particular update WSJT compiles again. I 
will suggest to make distro upgrade to Debian Testing from your recent 
Debian Stable 5. It is very easy using apt-get dist-upgrade. Debian 
Testing is as stable as Debian Stable IMHO. So nothing to worry about 
it. You will just have fresh software compared with Debian 5.

It was interesting that in the period of faulty compile all precompiled 
revisions of WSJT stopped working too. Now all they work again. So I do 
not think the problem was with compilers but with environment. Right now 
I use g95 as compiler although WSJT compiles well with gfortran too.
