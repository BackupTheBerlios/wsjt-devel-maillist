Hi John,

Thanks for the info.  I guess one size does not fit all; as 
we've seen in other ways, some combinations of 
python/numpy/f2py/gfortran behave differently from others.

I think there's no reason to keep the --debug flag, anyway. 
    As for "--noopt", that's exactly what was needed to make 
it all fly on Ubuntu 9.04.  For now, I guess, on your system 
you should edit it back to "--opt="-O".

I believe that if f2py calls f2py2e.py it implies that an 
earlier version of f2py is being used.  See
http://www.f2py.org/ and 
http://cens.ioc.ee/projects/f2py2e/usersguide/index.html .
