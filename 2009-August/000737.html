I now have (yet another) Mac available - MacBook Pro with fresh  
10.5.7 installation.  This provided a useful opportunity of  
installing the complete infrastructure to support WSJT compilation on  
a new system.    I have written a "Howto" which is now in Rev1320 of  
wsjt/trunk as "Wsjt-Howto-Mac_OS_X.html" which I hope will answer  
some of the queries that have been sent to me.   Please let me know  
if there are errors/omissions.

background colour problem?
