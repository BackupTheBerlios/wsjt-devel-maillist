> Question. I read somewhere, but can't recall where, that WSJT requires a specific sample rate
> of 44100, is this true?

WSJT uses 11025 Hz sampling, and therefore is happiest when the 
soundcard supports native sampling at 44100 Hz.  WSJT-X uses 12000 and 
48000 Hz.

> If I have .asoundrc set to 48000 is that going to cause issues for WSJT ?

Yes.
