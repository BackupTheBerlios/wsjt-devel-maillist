To:   Users of WSJT-X
From: Joe Taylor, K1JT, for the WSJT development team

We invite you to download and use the latest revision of WSJT-X: Version 
0.95, SVN revision 3243.  Changes from the previous release are
summarized in the ChangeLog, posted at

http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/wsjtx_changelog.txt

We consider something close to r3243 to be a candidate for release as
"Version 1.0" of WSJT-X -- probably within a week or two.  With this in
mind, please exercise this revision thoroughly and report any bugs or
important feature requests to us as soon as possible!

We draw your particular attention to the new WSJT-X User's Guide, posted at
http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/WSJT-X_Users_Guide.pdf .  We
are also very much interested in your comments on this Guide.  What is
unclear?  What is missing?  We will continue to update and add to this
guide, as time is available.

Please join me in sending special thanks to development team members
PY2SDR, VK4BDJ, AC6SL, and AF5X -- each of whom has made recent
important contributions to the design, code, documentation, or
reliability of WSJT-X.

With best wishes,

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
	For the WSJT development team
