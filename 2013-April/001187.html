Welcome, we'll be delighted to have you as part of the WSJT development 
team!  Your experience in Alpha testing and detailed reporting of bugs 
will be a real asset.

You should register at Berlios and get a login name there.  Most of us 
use our callsigns.  When you have a login name, let me know what it is,
