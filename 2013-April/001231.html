One problem I just discovered: If I try to change bands to a band the my
radios does not have, wsjtx crashes.

icom_set_freq: ack NG (0xfa), len=1
Qt has caught an exception thrown from an event handler. Throwing
exceptions from an event handler is not supported in Qt. You must
reimplement QApplication::notify() and catch all exceptions there.

terminate called after throwing an instance of 'RigException'
Aborted (core dumped)

I will take a look at this issue. Do you think it would be valuable to have
