WSPR r2285 introduces a new, "less random" algorithm to be used with 
coordinated band hopping.

In the coordinated hopping mode, a full cycle of 10 bands is completed 
every 20 minutes.  Thus, in any 2-hour sequence each activated band will 
be visited at least 6 times.  The Rx/Tx algorithm in r2285 guarantees 
that if pctx>0 at least 1 and no more than 3 of those six visits will be 
a Tx cycle.
