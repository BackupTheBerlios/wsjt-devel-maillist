Hi Joe & Co

I'm now involved with a system similar to WSPR for propagation research. We transmit 2000 baud BPSK Barker-13 codes every few minutes on one of seven frequencies outside the amateur bands. We have seven transmit sites and a 7.5 minute transmission cycle.

We too use auto ATUs and amateur grade transceivers. We use a 0.5 second carrier just before the main transmission of two seconds of BPSK to tune the ATU ready. It seems to be adequate.

Our receivers are somewhat special though, at $50,000 a throw, not easily reproduced. They do give us the arrival azimuth and elevation of the signals though.
