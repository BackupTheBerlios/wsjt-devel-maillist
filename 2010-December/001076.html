A brief follow-up to my previous message.  I just checked the present 
contents of ALL_WSPR.TXT in my working WSPR directory.  Here are the 
relevant statistics for the number of decodes with N < Limit, where N is 
the number of cycles through the sequential decoder, per message bit.

Limit   Decodes  Pct
---------------------
10000    2104    100%
  1000    1941     92
   100    1858     88
    10    1757     84

In other words: by reducing the variable "limit" from 10000 to 1000, 
thereby speeding up the decoding process by 10 times under worst-case 
circumstances, I would have lost 8% of my valid decodes.  Even at 
limit=100, I would lost only 12% of my decodes.   These numbers are typical.
