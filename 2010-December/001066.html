Hi Alex,

I am not sure that I understand just what you have done, and why.  The 
normal commands to compile WSJT in Linux, after checking out source code 
from the SVN repository, are

$ ./configure --enable-gfortran              (or "--enable-g95")
$ make

This assumes that you have already installed the following pre-requisites:

  python2.5-dev
  python-numpy
  python-imaging-tk
  python-pmw
  libportaudio2
  libportaudio-dev
  libsamplerate0-dev
  libfftw3-dev
  g95                      (optional)

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
