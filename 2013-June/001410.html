Hi all,

Thanks to John and John (AC6SL and G4KLA) for the code updates.  Those 
improvements are now part of SVN revision 3418, and -- assuming that 
problems don't turn up in the next few days -- I think this makes a 
reasonably stable candidate for WSJT-X v1.1.

Please give this version a try and let me know how it works for you.  I 
realize, of course, that many rigs don't offer a USB filter wider than 
2.4 - 2.7 kHz.  But even without the wideband possibilities, having both 
JT65 and JT9 available should be a big plus for many operators.

When you report some results, it will help if you mention what bandwidth 
you were using.

	-- Joe, K1JT
