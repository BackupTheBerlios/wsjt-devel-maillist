Hi Murray,

You have it almost right.  After your svn checkout, be sure to do

$ mkdir wsjtx_install

Then proceed as you did.  (I just did these exact steps on Ubuntu, to be 
sure it worked.  It does.)

When you start the program, be sure to follow steps in the Quick Start 
guide to v1.1, available at
http://www.physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/WSJT-X_1.1_Quick_Start.pdf

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
