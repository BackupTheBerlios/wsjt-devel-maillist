Hi
I've just joined this list and I'm keen to get wsjtx with jt65 working 
on Linux.

I've checked out r3389 and r3426 (current) and neither will decode the 
jt9 wav files that came with the windows r3323 installation. The 
waterfall just displays green noise, no obvious signals and of course 
there is no decode.

I can however correctly decode these files in wsjtx v1.0 r3348 (built 
from svn on the same Linux system).

I'm on Mint 14 (which is the same or very similar to Ubuntu 12.10?). My 
build steps are:

svn co svn://svn.berlios.de/wsjt/branches/wsjtx_k1jt_p1
edit wsjtx.pro otherwise the linker complains about 'gen65' (or similar, 
from memory!)
     - LIBS += ../wsjtx/lib/libjt9.a
     + LIBS += ../wsjtx_k1jt_p1/lib/libjt9.a
make -f Makefile.linux in lib/
qmake and make
