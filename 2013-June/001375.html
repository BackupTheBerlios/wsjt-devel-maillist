A few last-minute details before I must leave.

For those of you who are compiling WSJT-X for yourselves, and have read 
the Quick Start Guide posted at
http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/WSJT-X_1.1_Quick_Start.pdf

Copies of the sample files mentioned there have been posted at

http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/130418_1742.wav
http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/130610_2343.wav

A Linux executable kvasd (compiled on Ubuntu 12.04) is posted at

http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/kvasd

This program is executed from within the Fortran subroutine extract.f90. 
  Depending on how you install things, you might want to adjust the 
system call that starts it:

   iret=system('./kvasd -q > dev_null')

If anyone needs kvasd for OS X, G4KLA has a copy.

That's all from here until after June 22...
