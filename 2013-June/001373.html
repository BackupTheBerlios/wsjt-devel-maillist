Dear Colleagues,

WSJT-X v1.1, beta r3385 works well at my station: I can now make JT65 
and JT9 QSOs, alternating between modes as desired, with nothing more 
than mouse clicks.  This program version is ready for somewhat wider 
testing.

A "Quick Start Guide" outlining the new features and covering most of 
the differences from version 1.0 is posted at
http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/WSJT-X_1.1_Quick_Start.pdf .

A Windows installation package is posted at
http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT/WSJTX_110r3385.exe
For Linux and OS X, you can compile your own from the SVN repository.


No doubt you will find some things that don't work right in your setup. 
  Please let me know whet they are...  bug reports, suggested changes, 
and patches are all very welcome!

Note, however: I will be away from home and mostly out of email contact 
from tomorrow until June 22.

I hope to be able to make an open release of version 1.1 by around the 
end of June.

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
