Yes, the sensitivity is very good.  However, in cases where one or more 
strong signals are within +/- 1000 Hz of the one being decoded (and 
perhaps certain other situations that also don't occur in EME usage) the 
JT65 S/N values computed in r3391 and earlier are NOT reliable.  JT65 
cannot decode signals with actual S/N below about -25 dB.

A better S/N estimator was introduced today in r3392.  Further tests 
(and perhaps further improvements) are planned.
