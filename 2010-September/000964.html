Hi Zilvinas,

Happy to hear that you have made the desired wave file.

Time sync with WSPR signals is an all-or-nothing issue.  If you get a 
decode, it will be with full sensitivity.  The permissible range of time 
offsets goes from DT = -2 to +5 seconds, approximately.  Normally I tell 
people that it's best to keep timing accurate to +/- 2 seconds.

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
