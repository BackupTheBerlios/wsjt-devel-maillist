I have received reports that WSJT-X fails on Macs with the new OS X (10.9 aka Mavericks).  After investigation I find a message from Qt 5.1.1 (latest version) in qsystemdetection.h that Qt 5.1.1 does not support Mac OS X > 10.8.    So don't try!.
