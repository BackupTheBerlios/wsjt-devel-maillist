Thanks for cobbling together a Vista fix so quickly.  I'm running it 
now, and it appears to work properly in my Win7 machine with either

Audio\DisableInputResampling=false

or

[Tune]
Audio\DisableInputResampling=true

present in wsjtx.ini at program startup.  (I haven't yet looked at the 
code, but I presume that in Win7 "true" means that it will use 12 kHz 
sampling?
