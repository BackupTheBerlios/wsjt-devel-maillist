I had related rigctld issue in wspr4.0 svn version:
There were no id=2 in device list...
in wspr.py I manually changed "1901" (deprecated but probably still used 
by someone)  to "2"  in if/else branch and now at least band 
switching/hopping works.
I am not programmer but probably there's should be additional if/else 
for networked rigctl case (id's 1901 and 2 - in these cases only host 
name/ip and rig id are needed as parameters for rigctl omitting all the 
rest)
I could help to test/debug these issues if needed.
