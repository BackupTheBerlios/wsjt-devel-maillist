Hi Silvan,

Having thought a bit more about this, I agree that "some  
randomization is necessary" to avoid collisions in band-space.    But  
how to do this given the dual, and separate, responsibilities of  
wspr.py (band choice) and wspr2.f90 (tx/rx choice) needs some  
thought.    Some communication between wspr and wspr2 (via a common  
block) might help in decision making.   (In an earlier test I found  
that TX on 15m was avoided to such an extent (combination of band  
choice and wspr2 rx) that I began to think there was a bug  
somewhere.    A poor choice of sliders, perhaps.)

Getting more feedback from wspr'eres is needed.    The system as it  
is provides a very useful additional feature.
