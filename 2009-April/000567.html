Dear Colleagues,

In case you wonder what is going on in a new branch "WSJT8": 
  I am using this branch for some tests of several entirely 
new modes that *might* turn out to be worthy of inclusion in 
a released version of WSJT.

Very tentatively, the experimental modes are:

I-Scat: designed for ionospheric scatter at 50 MHz.
         (possible replacement for JT6M)

M-Scat: designed for meteor scatter at 144 MHz.
         (possible replacement for FSK441)

JT64:   designed for EME
	(possible replacement for JT65)

I will probably work on I-Scat first.

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
