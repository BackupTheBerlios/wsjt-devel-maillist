/usr/lib/python2.4/site-packages/numpy/distutils/cpuinfo.py
As nocona enables sse3 instructions for nocona, we should do a check for
sse3 in the _is_Nocona test routine.
I have posted a bug on the gentoo bug tracker, so hopefully it will go
upstream.
http://bugs.gentoo.org/show_bug.cgi?id=169262
