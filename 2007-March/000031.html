Hi,

I've unhooked the build of our copy of portaudio v19. There are two new
configure arguments to point at where the headers and libs are
for portaudio.

./configure --with-portaudio-include-dir=<path> \
--with-portaudio-lib-dir=<path> \
--enable-portaudio

As always, please check that things still build properly for you, they
build properly here.

Note, the API is different from V19 and V18 of portaudio, one such
difference is the number of arguments in Pa_OpenStream. Where various
OS put their variant version of portaudio v19 is highly variable.

I feel there is the possibility that a "snapshot" from svn of a v19 portaudio
may break the build of wsjt, so I personally feel more comfortable
having a copy we know works with wsjt. Users building wsjt can choose
to use our known working snapshot or not.
