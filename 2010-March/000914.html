For those who are interested in running WSJT on a Mac with Snow  
Leopard (Mac OS X 10.6.2) you will find an updated guide in the WSJT  
repository from revision 1809 onwards.    Mac OS X 10.6.2 is  
significantly different from previous versions of OS X (10.5 and  
earlier) since, by default, compilations on 10.6 are 64-bit (as is  
Python) and not 32-bit.   For the moment it seems that the 32-bit  
version of gfortran is more stable and so the build I have  
recommended in the guide is for this architecture.   If you download  
r1809 you will find  Wsjt_Howto_MacOSX_10.6.html to help you.   I  
have installed WSJT on an IMac and MacBook Pro and helped another  
person to get both WSJT and WSPR operational on 10.6.2.   (A guide  
for WSPR will be available soon.)
