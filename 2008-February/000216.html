Hi Ray,

thanks for the reply.

I finally tracked down the error:
Even if i include my linux pthread.h the local sched.h gets included
which is also a w32 header. If i rename both files to *_w32.h and write
this:

#ifdef Win32
    #include "pthread_w32.h"
#else
    #include <pthread.h>
#endif

to start_audio.c and also put the right include in sched_w32.h (l 288)
everything works fine.

The question that prevents me from applying this patch is: Did nobody
else see this or am i the first one to compile WSJT under GNULinux?
