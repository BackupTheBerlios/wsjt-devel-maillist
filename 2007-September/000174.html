Harry --

Please treat these new modes as experimental only.  You should not 
assume that anything in r237 right now will stay in the program.  In no 
sense has JT2 or JT4 been "released" for general use.  The large number 
of JT4 submodes, in particular, are there for testing purposes only.

Yes, using a Class C amplifier PSK modes can cause excessive spectral 
broadening.

It's good that you can compile the program.  No surprises there.  These 
modes are not yet ready for general on-the-air use.

	-- Joe, K1JT
