Reading hard about PSK I want to express my feelings against any PSK in
WSJT program. Speaking with other hams proved my thoughts. Most of us
are just on the edge of TVI. PSK will ruin neighbour TV sets. Beside
that PSK is not so easy to adjust as a transmitting level. ANd the last.
One must use low efficient linear amplifier. In contrast FSK works with
high efficient nonlinear amplifiers, no TVI and heat problems. Transmit
signal level is very easy to be adjusted. So I vote for FSK modes only.
IMHO any PSK will degrade overal WSJT quolity.
