Subject: WSJT 5.9.7 (r383)
Date:    May 4, 2007

WSJT Version 5.9.7 (r383) is now available for free download 
from the WSJT Home Page,
http://physics.princeton.edu/pulsar/K1JT.  It should appear 
soon on the European mirror site, http://www.dk5ya.de, as well.

Some of the changes from Version 5.9.6 (r309) are motivated 
by the recent explosion of activity using JT65A on the HF 
bands.  Among the new features are band settings for all HF, 
VHF, and UHF bands; a "Quick decode" menu item especially 
useful for terrestrial QSOs; double-right-click on callsign 
to produce Tx messages with callsigns and numerical signal 
reports; and optional Tx frequency offset at the value of 
Freeze DF.

A more complete list of program changes is available at 
http://pulsar.princeton.edu/~joe/K1JT/UpdateHistory.txt .

Installable binaries for Linux (in .deb and .rpm format) 
will be available soon.  WSJT also runs on FreeBSD and 
Macintosh OS/X.

As always, we would like to have your feedback -- good or 
bad! -- on the new version of WSJT.

                 -- 73, Joe, K1JT
