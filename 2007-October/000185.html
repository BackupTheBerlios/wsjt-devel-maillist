Hi Alberto,

You can edit the file "wsjtrc" to change the fonts used on 
the user interface screens.  Mine is appended below, for 
reference.  You can change to different font names, as well 
as different sizes.  The best ones to use will depend on the 
installed fonts on your system.

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT

####################### wsjtrc at K1JT ####################

*font:                  Arial 10
*Label*font:            Arial 10
*Text*font:             "Courier New" 12
*Canvas*font:           "Lucida Console" 16
*background:            gray85
*Text*background:       white
*Entry*background:      white
*foreground:            black
*Listbox*foreground:    RoyalBlue
