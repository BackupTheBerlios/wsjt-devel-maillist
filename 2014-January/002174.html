Hi John,

Thanks for sending (off list) the screen shot and wav file.  I'm sorry 
to say that I presently have no idea what may be causing your occasional 
glitch-ridden Tx audio.

I suppose you must be sending Tx and Rx audio back and forth between 
WSJT-X and PowerSDR (or OpenHPSDR mRX) by means of VAC lines, right? 
It's hard to say where the problem might be, in that chain.  It's clear 
that something is causing interruptions (and therefore abrupt phase 
jumps) in the audio stream.  Have you tried sending the Tx audio to the 
ANAN 100D through a real audio device and a physical cable, rather that 
through VAC?

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
