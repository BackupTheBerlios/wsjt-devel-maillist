Hi all, and especially AC6SL and G4KLA:

When you get a chance to package WSJT-X v1.3 for Linux and OS X, please 
do your build from SVN revision 3673.  The released programs will then 
be the same over all platforms.

If anybody would like to volunteer to make additional packages -- say, 
for FreeBSD, of orther commonly used Linux distributions, please step 
forward and let us know!

Also, if you have an opinion on the best way to handle distribution of 
the non-open-source kvasd, let us know that too.  Any way we do it, it's 
a pain -- but thankfully a rather minor one.
