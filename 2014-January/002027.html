David --

For example, in the line:
170800  9  -10  0.1  -46   7 *    GB3CSB IO75XX     1***** A

The * after the 7 indicates that the message contained the "normal" sync 
pattern for JT4.  (Messages containing a signal report use an inverted 
sync pattern.)

The ***** near line end is the result of a "quality" estimator being 
numerically too large to be printed in 5 columns.  (You're copying a 
very strong signal.)

For interest, send me a *.wav file containing a record that decodes with 
the ***** symbols.  I'd like to look ate it.

	-- 73, Joe, K1JT
