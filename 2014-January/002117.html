These multiple decodes with large signal level differences and equal 
spacings either side of the fundamental signal are most likely to be 
generated in the TX station audio stages. +/- 120Hz, +/-240Hz, ... and 
~20dB down or more are a common case, as you have there. Mixing with PSU 
line hum would seem to be an obvious culprit.
