Maybe you did not set Rx Frequency to 1224 Hz, as instructed??  Use the 
spinner control just above the Tx=Rx button.

> Also, the red and green markers
> at the top of the wide graph do not move. I tried exiting the program and
> starting the load/decode over and that did not fix it.

Clicking anywhere on the waterfall moves the green marker to that 
frequency.  Ctrl-click moves both green and red markers.

Double-click on a decoded text line moves the green and red markers to 
the frequency of that signal, UNLESS it's someone calling MyCall (and 
therefore highlighted in red).  In that case, only the green marker is 
moved.
