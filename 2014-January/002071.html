I've been running 1.2.1 r3590 for some time ... and there were a couple of
days where I had a rash of them at the home station ... and one day where I
had about a half-hour similar problem with crashing at the NC1I station.
This would seem to rule out computers; the setups were pretty much the same:
using a microHAM device for audio and rig control and FTDX5000.

My gut feeling is the crashes were attributed to something being decoded
being sent by a specific station as it was fairly consistent for a period of
time that I was operating (normally evenings UTC-0500 on 7.076 MHz) - QSY or
going QRT and coming back later never had the problem.  And FWIW - I have
not seen a crash now in some time.  (Offending station not being heard?!)
