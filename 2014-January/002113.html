The Show DXCC suggestion logic will false identify countries when users
modify the standard WSJT CQ sequence:

CQ MYCALL MYGRID

To wit:

CQ DX MYCALL - I want DX
CQ MA MYCALL - I want Massachusetts

Maybe a slight change in the parsing logic could help?  After finding the CQ
if the next item delimited by spaces is 3 characters or less then throw it
away and continue with the remaining string.  Excepting 3-character special
event calls this should help with correctly identifying the calling station
and its country.
