Bill, John --

Bill, in answer to your question: yes, summing groups of 4 samples is a 
low-pass filter of sorts.  It's just not a very good one.  Voltage 
attenuation for it goes as sin(x)/x (x is frequency) -- so in-band 
ripple is rather large and the aliased high-frequency components are not 
attenuated by very much.

I'll make a new LPF-and-downsample routine similar to fil1() but with a 
higher f_cutoff and somewhat smaller stopband attenuation.  I'll keep it 
simple for now, just a single channel.  (It will be easy to generalize 
it if/when we need to use it for two or more channels.)

Incidental question: what is the store() function, line 52 in 
Detector.cpp?  I can't see where it is defined.

	-- Joe
