wsjtx_1.1.r3496 has finally been accepted as a debian package.

It failed to build on some of the platforms, because
lib/Makefile.linux specified "CFLAGS ... -mno-stack-arg-probe"  This
-mno-stack-arg-probe option is not recognized on several platforms.

Google search showed that it has to do with alloca(), but I do not see
that used anywhere. I suspect that this was originally in
lib/Makefile.mingw, and was then copied into lib/Makefile.linux

I removed this option, rebuilt, reinstalled, and I am still able to
decode the sample .wav file. This change is included in what was
checked in to debian.

I propose to remove this option from lib/Makefile.linux in the most
current source. Does anyone have any objection? Does anyone remember
why this option was present?
