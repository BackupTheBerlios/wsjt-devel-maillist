Hi All,

attached is a proposed revised configuration dialog. I would appreciate 
any feedback.

You can give it a try in QtDesigner by loading it and pressing 
Alt+Shift+R. Obviously the bare UI isn't functional but comments on 
layout, suitability, wording, typos in the tool tips, grouping, 
ordering, missing items, etc. are welcome.

If used, it would be coupled with a much improved radio interface that 
could include setting mode and split automatically as well as supporting 
OmniRig as a new way of interfacing to radios and much improved HRD 
interfacing.

73
Bill
G4WJS.
