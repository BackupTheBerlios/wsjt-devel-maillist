I ran wsjtx 3680 for a few hours and it did not exit by itself.

I then noticed the transmit audio was gone.
This appears to be caused by the Nvidia graphics driver.

Sooo... I reinstalled Heisenbug this time WITHOUT the Nvidia driver.
At first I was having problems with the serial port until I realized
I had not uninstalled Fedora's ModemManager.  ModemManager
is an undocumented fascist bagbiter that assumes serial ports are
connected to - you guessed - modems.  How quaint. I uninstalled ModemManager
and wsjtx is happier.   Other Linux distros may have a similar port minder
which can throw a spanner in the works.  Dialup modems are so yesterday.

I plan on leaving wsjtx running as much as possible (modulo Super Bowl)
for the next day or so.
