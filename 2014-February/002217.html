Hi, Joe,

Those were early days examples of what could be done or how AsciiDoc
could be of use. They "should be" modified to suite whatever the real
need is.

What triggered the whole rig-config thing was, I constantly see
questions asking  "How do I configure X,Y,Z" rig / interface with
WSJT-X, so I thought maybe that would be something useful to the users
and used it as an example.

I have no issues with pulling them all out, sure makes the build
faster :-) ..

I think there is value it it somewhere, just maybe not the way it's
currently being presented.
