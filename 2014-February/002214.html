Hi Chase, Greg, and all,

We are not going to provide hand-holding that tries to cover every 
possible combination of radio, interface device, connection path, etc.

As I see it, though, it may be useful to provide a list of settings 
known to work well with particular radios.

I don't understand the reason for including come of the items presently 
in the "rig-config-template" document.

The callsign of the person submitting the information might make sense, 
but why include "MyGrid"?

In a page for, say, the Kenwood TS-2000, why include such user-optional 
things as whether or not to activate PSK Reporter, CW ID after 73, Tx 
Split, etc?

Why have entries for PTT method, CAT port, Audio devices, etc., which 
depend on a user's interface device and computer setup, but not on the 
radio type?
