Hi Dave,

> I've managed tobreak it.
> 
> I was messing with the Tune facility, set the slider to 100 (which I thought 
> meant the rig would TX for 100 seconds) and pressed the Tune button.  The 
> rig went into TX (PTT enabled) but no power out seen, so I guess the Tune 
> doesn't enable any audio to the rig?

You must have selected an invalid or inoperative audio 
output device.  "Tune" keys the PTT and sends a fixed 1500 
Hz audio tone for a number of seconds given by the Tx% slider.

> I'll try uninstalling and reinstalling to see if that cures the problem.

There is seldom a good reason to uninstall and reinstall 
WSPR.  If you want a clean start, just delete WSPR.INI.
