Hello David,

for the messages
bt_audio_sevice_open...

Remove with Synaptic all bluetooth , stuff  (unless you need it, then 
you will have to live with it!:) )

For the rest, play around, do not use oss, use alsa by not selecting 
/dev/dsp, instead use
for example
0 'your sound card' (hw:0,0) something like that in the drop down box.

before your re-start wspr, check with
arecord -d 2 test.rec
if it does not come back after 2 sec, then ..... reboot .... seems like 
alsa device is busy and I did not find any other way to 'unlock' the 
input device,

Good  luck,
