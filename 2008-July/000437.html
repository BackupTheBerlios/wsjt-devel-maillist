I apologize for all the emails regarding the Mac work I've been doing.  
Issue resolved.

What a waste of several hours between last night and today!  The culprit 
turned out to be a gfortran problem...I turned it into a heisenbug by 
putting in lots of debugging print statements, and it suddenly started 
decoding on the G5! The symbol array was not getting stored correctly 
from decode162 to inter_mept (unless I printed the values, of course!).  
No one to blame but myself; in my haste to get a new toolchain in place 
last night after punting macports, I looked for packaged binaries for 
gfortran and didn't realize I got a pre-release version.  I replaced it 
with a stable 4.2.3, reverted to the wspr branch I checked into svn 
earlier today, and it all works.

So never mind the reports about WSJT issues....I don't have time to try 
it again now, but no reason to take it seriously until it can be reproduced.

Now WSPRing 30m from the G5: Txing, Rxing, and posting to the database.
