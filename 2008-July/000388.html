Hi all
I've found a nasty buglet in MDV2008.1 x86_64.
I've verified its a distro issue by trying similar apps run in either
Wine or CXoffice. And on another distro which I'm now using.
Its appeared very recently and the symptoms are that apps that use a
waterfall display show a continuous change in frequency,
ie put a single tone in and it will be shown drifting.

I don't think its a fftw problem as that has not changed on issue
number for a few months. And it probably is only likely to appear on
Ham apps, as not many other users use similar software.
Where a drift is not obvious the frequency appears about 1/4 of what
it should be, so on wsjt its right at the lower edge.

HTH
