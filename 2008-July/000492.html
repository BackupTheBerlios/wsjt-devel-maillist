For what it's worth:

Today I did a fresh installation of Ubuntu 8.04 (actually 
the Kubuntu flavor) and compiled WSJT and WSPR from r976.

I found both programs to work correctly when compiled with 
g95.

Both programs compile OK with gfortran, but on startup they 
execute an illegal instruction and die.  I have not yet 
identified the cause of the error, although my present guess 
is that it has something to do with getting threads started. 
  (I have no idea why this should be any different with the 
two compilers, though.)
